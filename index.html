The 3% Pathway

HOW IT WORKS

01
Intake & Evaluation

Film review, GPA assessment, location interests, and play style analysis. We evaluate where you can realistically thrive — not where you think you should be.

02
Strategy Session

A first meeting with athlete and parent. Honest evaluation, realistic pathways, clear expectations, and an assigned consultant — you leave with a real action plan.

03
Active Exposure

Film distributed to targeted coaches that match your evaluation. Personalized outreach — not mass blasts. Social media strategy and direct network introductions.

04
Ongoing Support

Weekly check-ins, monthly progress reviews, academic tracking, and communication coaching. We stay in the fight with you from intake to commitment.
